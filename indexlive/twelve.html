Sachchi samman



Jis tarah desh me anek party ke adhyaksh chunav ladte hai.
badi party to nahi thi, par ek nek dil party thi, jinka nam tha lokpa arthath lok kalyan party.
is party ke adhyaksh the keshab , usne aaj tak jitne bhi chynav huye ek me bhi nahi lada.
log sochte the lokpa ek party hai par ye chunav kyu nahi ladta.
ek din keshab ji bhashan de rahe the tabhi usne kaha ki "lokpa matlab log kalyan party. aap sab itne salo se soch rahe the ki me chunav nahi rad raha hu to suniye mera maksad hai garibo ki madad karna, jo insan madad karna chahta hai use yesa to nahi ki chunav jitna hi hoga. jo achchha kam karna chahta hai vo yese bhi kar sakta hai".
aage kaha "me kabhi chunav nahi ladunga me sirf logo ki madad karunga.
lokpa ek mahan sangrathan hai jo bina svarth ke logo ki madad karta hai, agar aapki koi dikkat hai to aap hamare office call kijiye hamare log aapke ghar jake aapke presani dur kasrenge ".
itna kahakar keshab chala gya.
lokpa garibi ki madad to karta par jo es ilake me chunav jita hai, vo sarkar se aane vali rashi ko kha jata hai.
lokpa me karykarta kam hai isliye unsab chicho par dhyan jyada nahi de pata.
lokpa ka maksad aas pas ke sare gaon ko develop karna tha par kuch badmas neta ki vajah se logo ko sharab ka lat lag gya.
un logo ke liye vahi bhagwan ban gya, par vo samajh nahi rahe the ki unhi ka paisa lut kar unko de raha hai.
mene bhi dekha hai jab election hota hai achchhe logo ko chhor kar bure log hi jeet jate hai.
ve log logo ko paisa deta hai, aur chand paiso me bik jate hai aur use hi agla neta bana deta hai.
insan apne aap ko itna kam aakta hai ki apni hi muly ko bhul jata hai.
yese hi din bitne lage 6 mahine bad sabne mitting ki aur gaon ke develop ke liye fesala liya.
jisme kaha gya ki har insan ki alag alag taklif hoti hai har ek insan ki taklif jankar uske hi hisab se ham uski madad karenge.
sabhi gaon ke har ghar ghar jakar pata lagane lage ki kiska kya taklif hai.
jab sare data collection hop gya. tab dekha gya ki presani to hai par har ghar me do-tin beroj gar hai.
par hum to koi company nahi hai jo fectory khole ya logo ko kam de, jo logo ki samsaya thi use ham 3 mahine solve kar diye aur jo hamare samne sabse badi presani thi ki beroj gari use kese thik kare.
hamne ek fir naya faisla kiya ki jitne bhi bachhe hai un sab ko coding sikhanyenge vo bhi free taki har ek insan apna kuch bana sake aue kuch naya kar sake .
hamne gaon jakar logon se kaha ki ham bachho ko computer class karbayenge sab log man gye par hamare pas time ki dokkat thi koi ek teachers itne sare bachho ko kese badayega aur bachhe kis time free hoge hame kese pata chalega uske liye gaon ke andar hi hamne night class open kiya jisse bachho ki apne ghar ke samne hi class kiya jaye aur yesa hi huaa .
sare bachho ko hum coding sikhane lage aur use life practicle article kese likhe jaye , page kese banaye, website kese banaye, sare chicho ko sikhane lage.
aur bachchhe bhi isme bahut enjoy karne lage .
sabhi bachchho ko online paisa kamane ke bahut sare tariko ko bataya aur aaj bahut sare log bachchhe ghar bethe hi computer ki madad se paisa kamane lage.
logo ne jo hame di wo thi samman jo asli samman tha. bachho se lekar buro tak hamre kam ko dekhkar hame ijjat dete.
hame aur chahiye hi kya .
Thanks .
itni samman dene ke liye.
