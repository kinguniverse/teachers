Gyan ki shakti



Kisi gaon me jiten nam ka ek kisan rahta tha. ve bade hi prisarami tha . par vah vyarth bakbas aur jhagre karne ke karan log use buri najaro se dekhta tha.
jo samay use khali milta vah vyarth bolna shuru kar dete the. jisse log usse irsya karte . log use ahankari samajhte.
vo choti choti bato se jhagra karne lag jata tha. iske under krod bahut jyada tha .
ek bar vah kisi gaon kisi kam ke liye gaye.
usne dekha ki ek kisan hal jodh raha the, vah achchhe se hal joth nahi pa raha tha .
jiten ko yah dekhkar dya aa gyi aur vah hal jothne me madad karne lage.
chilchilahat dhup me dono khet ke kam karne lage .
jiten ne uski niswarth madad ki isliye vah kisan jiten ki khub tarif karne lagi.
par jiten ki vah aadat thi vyarth bolne ki aur vah khud apna tarif karne laga jisse logo ko bura lagne laga.
tab ek vyakti ne majakiy andaj se kaha "isne kya kiya isse achchha to me kar sakta hu" , yah sunkar jiten ko gussa aa gya aur us aadmi se lad gya .
ye sab dekhkar log jiten se irshya karne lage kyuki vyarth bakbas ke sath jhagra bhi karne laga .
jiten ki sari mehnat apni vyarth bakbas ke karan pani fer gya.
jiten is jhagre se chintit rahne lage , sochne lage itna kuch karke bhi log mujhe bura kahte hai .

mano jiten ke bhagy khul gye, vah ghar pe so rahe the tabhi darvaje par kisi ke aane ka aavaj sunai di , jiten uthkar darvaja khula to dekha ek pandit ji lal chiga ud kar khade hai .
jiten ne swagat kiya aur ghar ke andar lakar ek achchhe aasan par bithaya. pandit ji ne bataya hai ki vah bahut dur se aaye hai. bahut thak chuke hai , din bhi dhal rahi hai.
aaj rat yahi rahne ki yojna banayi. garmi ka din tha jiten ne pandit ji ko khana parosa aur pankha kar raha tha.
tabhi jiten ne kaha " hai pandit ji " , jiten ne apni sari ghatna bata di . vah pandit se aagrah karne laga ki koi yesa rasta bataiye jisse log mujhe bura na kahe aur me ek achchha insan ban saku
tab pandit ji ne kaha "hai jiten yadi tum chahteb ho ki log tumhe tumhari bastavik yogta se bhi jyada yogy vyakti samjhe to tum kam bolo, buddhiman ki bat karne se adhik buddhiman pratit hona achchha hai, tum chup raho , kisi ke prashn kiye bina mat bolo, vyarth mat bolo, jab hum adhik bolte hai aur vyarth jhagra karte hai to hum apni hi shreshta ko kam kar dete hai , yadi tum chahte ho ki log tumhe samman de , tumse koi jhagra na kare, tumhe achchha kahe to tumhe bhi logo ko samman dena hoga, tujhe bhi kisi se jhagra nahi karni chahiye, yadi tum hamesa khus rahna chate ho to dusro ko bhi khusi ki parva karni chahiye, jab hum jhagra karte hai tab hum apni hi shreshta ko khatam kar dete hai , kisi ko marne se , kisi ko gali dene se apni hi samman kam ho jati hai.
isliye yadi tum chahte ho ki log tumhe samman de to tum chup raho. kuch mat bolo. "
tabhi se jiten kisi ko kuch nahi kahta sada chup rahtaq hai aur kisi se jhagra nahi karta. log uski maun ko dekhkar samajh gya ki jiten ab achchha ban gya
ek din jiten gaon ke chorahe par betha tha uske sath aur bhi log the , iske kuch dur aam ke ped tha .
kuch bachhe aam todne ke liye patthar fek rahe the tabhi ek patthar jiten ke mathe me laga aur khun bahne laga .
log dar gye kyuki jiten vese to bahut gusse vala tha par jiten ne kuch nahi kiya .
jiten ne bachhe ko bulaya aur kaha " yese patthar matn fekna kisi lag jayega achchhe bachhe yese nahi karte , jav ghar me padai karo"
tabhi ek vyakti ne kaha ki " jiten tu use chor diya" .
jiten ne kaha " vo ek chhota bachchha hai, uska koi galti nahi hai ".
yah sunkar jiten aur chakit rah gye, log sochne lage jo bekaran jhagra karta tha vo aaj itna achhe kese ban gye, jo sada burai karta tha uske ander achchhai ki niv kese panpi.
logo ko kuch jhamajh me nahi aa raha tha vo ye samajh raha tha ki jiten badal gya hai. ab achchha ban gya.
kuch din bad jiten vahi gaon gye kisi kam se, kam kar lot rahe the tabhi jor ki pyas lagi , vah pani ki talas me ghumne lage.
dekha ki ek ghar me do bhai jhagra kar rahe the. logo ki sankhya adhik thi.
usne socha vyarth me jhagra kyu sunu ye soch kar vo jane vala tha tabhi vahi use kua dikhayi diya, vah pani pine gye badkismti se jiten ko marne laga aur kahne laga "tumhari itni himmat ki tum mere kuye se pani lene lage".
tab jiten ne use dhire se kaha " hai manav yadi mujhe marne par tumhe shanti milti hai to mujhe aur maro, me tumhari man ki shanti ke liye ye dard shahne ko deyar hu."
yah sunkar wo aadmi bahut lajjit huye aur vo per pakar kar mafi magne laga aur kahne laga ki aaj se me kabhi jhagra kabhi nahi karunga .
sabhi log jiten ki gyan aur imandari dekhkar chakit rah gye aur unki man aur samman charo dishao me fel gyi.
