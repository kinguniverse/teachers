Tark se abgun



Manushy ko tark nahi karna chahiye, tark jhagre ka rup le leta hai.
jab ham tark karte hai tab ham apni hi gusse ko badate hai jisse hum khud ki aur dosro ko hani pahuchati hai.
tark karne se gussa utpan hota hai aur gussa utpan hone se hamare budhhi nast ho jati aur jab hamari buddhi nast ho jati hai, tab ham \ ahankari ho jate hai aur jab ahankar aa jata hai to ham galat hi karte hai.
jisse logo me apni shreshta kam ho jati hai.
tark ek bimari hai jo gussa utpan karta hai aur gusse me ham andar hi andar jalne lagte hai, jo hame andar se khokhla kar deta hai.
tark ek mahamari hai jo hamari samman ko kam kar deta hai.
gussa jab aa jata hai to ham ashubh vaky bolte hai jisse samaj me hamri shreshta kam ho jati hai.
tark mat karo kam bolo jisse hame apni vastvik yogta se bhi jyada yogta sanjhne lagaya.

Krodh Pe kabu

manushy ko chahiye ki apni krodh ko shant kar le, krodh manav jivan ke liye bahut badi khatra hai.
krodh se jo kam aarambh kiya jata hai uska ant lajja se hota hai kyuki krodh se dusre ko nicha dikhane ka faisla liya jata hai.
krodh ko dheere dheere vas me karna chahiye, krodh pe kabu pane ke liye kabhi tark na kare, tark se krodh aa jata hai.
isse achchha hai ki aap shant rahe aur samajh ki khel ko dekhte rahe.
krodh to ek dikhayi hai par iske pichhe hazaro avgun chhupe hote hai,jaise ahankar, lobh, irshya, aadi.
krodh hamari shanti bhang kar deti hai aur hame durso ko hani pahuchane ke liye aage badati hai.
mahatma goutam buddh ne kaha tha ki "jis tarah lakdi me aag lag jane se lakdi khud jal jati hai, usi tarah jab manushy me krodh aata hai to vo svany andar hi andar jalta hai".
jo samman se garvochit nahi hota, apman se gussa nahi hota bure kathan nahi kahta vahi shreshta sadhu hai.
jab ham man hi man mandir me jakar bhagwan se magte hai tab hum sochte hai ki hamne jo kuch bhi kaha vo sab bhagwan ne sun liya, par jab hum kisi ko gali dete hai ya kisi ke bare me bura kahte hai ya sochte hai tab kya bhagwan nahi sunta.
har insan me bhagwan birajman hai kisi ko bekar bura na kahe mithi vani bole aapka kalyan hoga.
krodh ki aandhi se vivek ko nast kar deta hai.

krodh ko kabu kese kare

jis tarah talab ke gande pani ko hath se saf karne ke koshish karte hai to vo pahle se jyada ganda ho jata hai, us pani ko kuch der yese hi chhor do aur dekhna kuch der bad gandgi niche beth jayegi aur saf pani upper aa jayega , yesa hi hota hai krodh me jab koi insan jyada krodh me ho to use akele rahne do vo kuch der bad khud hi shant ho jayega.
apne andar utpan hone vali gusse ko samjho man hi man gusse ko pahchano ki mejhe gussa aa raha hai aur use control karne ki chesta karo.
krodh se ham vinas karte hai, krodh insano ke man samman ko mar deta aur samaj me use ek bekar insan ke rup me dekha jata hai.
jiska savbhav nindaniy hai vo murkh gun me bhi dos dekhta hai, adharmik bure vachan bolne vala aur krodhi, yese manushy par sankat gheere rahte hai.
jo log vartman samay par dhyan kendrit karte hai use bahut badi rahashya ka pata chalta hai.
apne jivan ko aaj badlo kal ka jua mat khelo.
yah sansar bhagwan ka rach ek kitab hai jo prakriti ko smajh leta hai vo is duniya ki bahut badi rahsya ka pata laga leta hai.
aapas me achchhe sambandh rakho, dusre se achchhe vyabhar karo, saty kaho ye achchhe manushy ka gun hai.
chhota chhota kam bhi mahan hota hai jaise ghas se banayi rassi se hathi bhi bandha ja sakta hai.
sabhi kahte hai pad kar achchhi nokri le lo koi yesa nahi kahta ki pad kar samaj ka bhala karo padayi kya apne liye aur paiso ke liye hoti hai.
me hazar din jina nahi chahta me ek yesa kam karna chta hu jise log hazar din yad rakhe.
manushy ko kam bolna chahiye vipatti me mon rahna chahiye yahi ati uttam kary hai.
kisi ke prash kiye bina na bole tatha kisi ke anyas se prash kare to bhi na bole shant rahe. yah mahan puruso ka gun hai.
yah atal saty hai ki ham hazar sal tapsya kar le, lakho dan puny kar le fir bhi bhagwan nahi milegi agar maa baba ko dhukraya to.
sukh sabko priy hai dukh sabko apriy ham sabhi jina chahte hai marna koi nahi chahta jis tahar ki vyvahar ham chahte hai vese hi dusro ko bhi karni chahiye.
kisi bhi pashu, pankshi aadi ko marna spast rup se amanviy hai.
