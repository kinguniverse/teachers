Thank Dost



Me Mirdha visv bidyalay me padta tha.
jo jivan hum shikshan kal me bitate vo bada hi diljasp hota.
Me bhi visv bidyalay ki padayi bhul nahi sakta. tab me b.com kar raha tha.
mere kamre me me aur mere dost rohan rahta thajise me pagal kahta tha kyuki jab bhi dekho vo kitab ko hi dekhta me use kahta itna padega to pagal ho gayega, par mere is bat par vo muskura deta. mujhe ajib sa lagta kyunki uski muskan ka bajah pata nahi chalta
jab bhi dekho hath me kitab rahta hi tha. padai me vah bahut hi diljasp tha aur padne me bahut tej tha.
sir jo saval puchhta uska jabab turant de deta mano use pahle se hi pata hai ki yahi puchne vala hai aur vahi batane vala hai.
vaha ham sab padte enjoy karte par vo sirf padai karta .
ek din visv bidyalay jane ke liye pagal nikla , tabhi mene pukara aur kaha 2 miniute ruk me bhi aa raha hu. vah ruk gya.
me jaldi se nahaya , khana khaya aur nikla tab tak der ho chuki thi.
pahuchte pahuchte thori der ho gyi class me teacher the . jaise hi teacher ne dekha aur kaha "Aaj itna der kyu huyi".
mene turant jabab diya ki rohan ki vajah se , rohan ko koi kuch nahi kahta agar me apna naam le leta to samajh jao kya hota .
hame bethne ke liye bola aur ham beth gye tbhi ek bachha dorta huaa sir ke pas aaya aur kahne laga "sir hamari english teacher english me bat karti hai, aap ganit padate ho to aap ganit ki bhasha me bat kyu nahi karte".
tab hamare ganit teacher ne kaha "yaha se jaldi 9 2 11 ho ja nahi to 2 2 laga dunga to thopde ki 12 baj jayega".
vah bachchha chala gya aur ham sab hasane lage.
chhutti ho gyi me aur pagal room aa rahe the. tab pagal ne pucha "tune meri shikayat sir se kyu kiya"
me chup raha kyuki iska jabab mere pas nahi tha. aakar kitab rakha aur pagal ko kahne laga chalo khelne par usne mana kar diya ur kaha ki use padai karna hai, mene kaha abhi to aaye padke aur kitna padega, usne jane se mana kar diya.
itna kahkar me khelne chala gya . sham ko khel kar aa gya muh hath dhone laga to dekha vo abhi bhi pad hi raha hai .
dekha kursi par bedhkar hath me kitab liye , man hi man kuch barbara raha hai , shayad kuch topic yad kar raha hai.
mujhe ajib sa lagta use dekhkar. us din mujhe yesa laga jaise mano vo mujhe kuch kahna chahta hai. vese to final year chal raha tha. mujhe koi tensaion nahi thi.
examation ke sirf 7 mahine hi bache the aur me football khelne me time barbad kar raha tha.

ek din me pagal ke sath subah 3:30 baje uth gya , pagal kitab lekar bethn gya aur me dorne ke liye medan chala gya , yah dekhakar pagal ko achchha nahi laga. ek achchha dost hone ke nate mujhe achchhi rah pe rane ki sochi jab me vapas aaya to mujhe kahta hai " yesa kyu karta hai ".
Mene puchha "kya".
tab usne mujhe vistar se samjhaya kaha "dekh tere pitaji ne tujhe padane ke liye veja hai , har mahine tujhe paisa deta hai, kabhi tune socha hai ki kitne mehanat majduri karke tujhe paisa deta hai, har bap chahta hai ki uska beta achchha ho par tu bigrte hi ja raha hai tujhe shayad pata nahi tere pitaji na jane kitne sapne dekhe hai tere liye, jab use pata chalega ki uska beta padta nahi to uske dil me kya bitega ,tu mera bat man aur tu aaj se padayi thik se kar, jab tu fail ho jayega to ghar valo ko kitna dard hoga ".
yah kahkar kursi par beth gya .
yesab sunakar me ja hi raha tha ki usne fir kaha "nast dhan mil jayega, bichhare mitr mil jayega, dusri patni mil jayenge, par ye manav jivan bar bar nahi milega".
ye bat sunkar mere dil ek lahar si dor pari mujhe khud pe gussa aane laga , uski ek bat ne mera jindgi badal di.
examation aa gya hum dono ne exam diya , vo pagal toper kar gya aur me 2nd pass ho gya.

Subah ke char baj gye the aur me so raha tha. tabhi pagal aaya aur chokane vali khabar kaha ki "me aaj ghar ja raha hu na jane kab mulakat hogi, shayad yah hamri aakhri aur antim mulakat ho ".
yah kahte hi mene gale laga liya.
subah ke 9 baj rahe the pagal apna saman bhar kar ja raha tha , tabhi usne mujhe dhire se kaha "Thanks Dost".
yah sunkar mere ankh se aasu aa gya .
