10 sal bad



Rat me ghumne ka maja hi kuch aur hota hai, aasman me tare chamk rahe, chand ki chandni kirne , bahti huyi hawaye mano jannat me aa gye.
us rat sadak par koi nahi tha, delhi ke lal kile me kuch karykarm chal raha tha isliye sadak sunsan tha meri police ki dyuti rat ko hi thi.
thand ka din tha, akele me achchha nahi lag raha tha , sadak ke sare dukane band ho gye the. tabhi meri najar sadak ke kinare ek dhaba khula huya tha.
me chala gya bijli nahi thi, dukan me andhera chhaya huaa tha, tabhi mujhe kuch girne ki aavaj sunayi di.
mene tarch mara to dekha ek aadmi kambal aurth kar betha huaa hai.
mene pucha "kon ho tum".
aadmi " me vivek hu".
me " tu yaha kya kar raha hai".
aadmi "me apna dost ka intjar kar raha hu, hum dono garib privar se the, dono ne nischay kiya tha ki hum kam karne jayenge, vo alag jagah chala gya aur me alag jagah chala gya, hum dono me bat huyi thi ki 10 sal bad hum isi jagah milenge aur aaj das sal pura huaa hai aur me intjar kar raha hu. usne kaha tha ki chahe kitne hi dur me rahe, chale kitni barish ho, chahe kuch bhi ho , hum dono yahi par milenge, mujhe pura yakin hai ki vo jarur aayega, vah aata hi hoga, yah sab sunkar aapko ajib sa lag raha hoga par yah saty hai"
kuch der bad ek mota, lamba sa aadmi aaya aur vivek vivek pukarne laga.
yah sunkar us aadmi ne kaha dekha mene kaha tha na ki vo jarur aayega.
dono gale lagne lage aur bite kal ki bate karne lage. ve dono bahut khus the
dono 10 sal me kya kiya vo batane lage aur me vahi betha huaa tha sun raha tha.
vivek ne kaha " tum badal gye ho dost".
usne kaha "nahi me pahle jaisa hi hu, par badal tum gye ho, me tumhari khabar rakhta tha aaj kal tum chori kyu karte ho, tujhe sharm nahi aati".
chori karke kya milega do pal ki khusi ke liye kisi ki mahine ki kamayi chhin leto ho.
insan kitne mehnat karke paisa kamate kya tum jante ho jo majduri karta hai vo pura din dhup me rahkar kam karta hai.
tumhe paiso ki kadar nahi to kya huaa insan ki kadar to karni chahiye.
agar tum chahte ho ki log tumhe ijat den to tum ye kam chhor do, me tumhare liye vaha ek nyi nokri dekhi hai. hum dono jayenge aur vahi kam karke aaram ki jindgi jiyenge.
kuch din bad vivek aur kam pe chale gye.
par vivek ko vah kam pasand nahi aaye kyuki vo bina mehnat ke kam karna pasand karta tha, jaise ki chori karta tha.
par kisi tarah usne vivek ko kam karne ke liye mana hi liya.
ek din vah betha huaa tha aur vivek aaya aur kahne laga "dost me aaj ghar jana chahta hu , mujhe kam pasand nahi aa raha hai".
usne kaha "thik hai, tum yaha se chale jao par ek bad hamesa se yad rakhana ki me tumhara dost hu aur me tumhe akele kahi nahi jane dunga, dekho agar tumhe yaha nahi rahna hai to koi bat nahi pahle mere bat dhyan se sun lo, mene 10 salo se yaha kam kiya hai kuch paise hai mere pas .
chalo hum dono ghar chalte hai aur apna ek chhoti si dukan kholenge , hum dono usi me kam karenge. "
ye bate sunkar vivek bahut khus huaa aur dono agle hi din bus pakra aur gaon chala aaya.
chhoti si pyari si kya naya dukan khola aur dono usi me kam karne lage ab dono bahut khus hai.
