Jab thak jao tab aaram kar lo par har mat mano.
Manushy ko dukh apne,lalsa aur bure karm ke anusar hota hai.
ye sansar dukho se gheera huaa hai, ye sansar dukho ka ghar hai hum ye bate isliye kah rahe hai ki manushy ki ichchha anant hai jo kabhi pura nahi hoga, ek ichchha ke pura hote hi dusra teyar rahta hai.
lalsa,irshya,bure karm hame maya jal me bandhe rakhta hai.
jisne apni ichchhao me kabu pa liya usne apne dukho ko jit liya.
hame jo kuch bhi mile use bina irshya kiye grahan kar lena chahiye yahi uttam rasta hai.
jo lena hai vo le lo jo dena hai vo de do isme irshya, ghamand aadi mat karo.
achchhe karm karte jao gyan apne aap aayenge.
Apne swabhav ke anusar unnati karta jao sanskar apne aap aayega.
apne man ko vash me karne ke liye apne me gambhir sawarup ko apnana chahiye.
Sabhi log jante hai ki ek din sabhi manushy ko marna hai.
log yah bhi jante hai ki khali hath aaye aur khali hath hi jayenge, jo karm hai jo insan ke marne ke bad bhi rahti hai, ishliye kahte hai achchhe karm karne chahiye.
hame vo bastu kamani chahiye jo sada hamare pas rahegi marne ke bad bhi vo hai hamara karm.
kisi ko bura kahne se achchha hai hum chup hi rahe.
saty ke raste pe chal kar dekh liya chot bahut lagti hai par dard nahi hoti.
bemani se prapt kiye dhan se jo dosh chhipaya jata hai vo chhipta nahi balki dusra naya dos utpanna ho jata hai.
jo log bado ka samman nahi karte, puja path nahi karte us insan ko sankat ghere rahte hai.
mon me vak sakti hota hai, mon us avasta ko kahte hai jab dhayan avasta sunay ho, jab khud ki dharkno ki aavaj sunayi dene lage.
man karta hai kahi dur chala jau jaha koi nahi ho.
aapas me achchhe sambandh banao , dusre ko mithe vani kaho aapka kalyan hoga.
manushy ki khusi aur dukh usne andar rahne vali lalsa ke karan hota hai.
dukh aane par khud ko sambhalo, khusiya aane par jyada dikhava mat karo, ye dono savbhavik hai aate jate rahte hai, kisi ki bhi ninda na kare.
mar mito apne laksh ko pane ke liye tum hi apna kam nahi karoge to kon karega, gyan se moksh prapt hota bhakti se isvar prapt hota hai.
yadi tum is duniya ko jitna chahte ho to pyar karna sikho kyunki ham is duniya ko goli bandhuk se nahi jit sakte jit sakte hai to sirf pyar se.
kamjori sabhi ki hoti hai, kamjoriya hame tab sata pati hai jab hamara dhyan laksh pe nahi hota, jab hamara dhyan laksh pe hota hai to sari kamjoriya gayab ho jati hai.
apni kamjori kisi ko mat batao kyunki isi ka fayda log udate hai.
aapko jo achchha lage vo karo par kabhi bhi bemani na karo.
har insan jina chahta hai par vahi insan markar bhi amar rahta hai jo dusro ki madad karta hai.
jab bhi moka mile uska fayda utha lo kyuki moka dubara nahi milta.
jivan jine ke liye ek laksh hona bahut jaruri hai aur apna laksh chuncha rakho taki tum mahan ban sako.
is prithvi me jo bhi hota hai, achchha bura ye sab bhagwan ka maya rupi khel hai isliye vyarth ki chinta chhor do aur aage badte raho.
manav jivan 84 lakh yoniyo me ek bar milta hai, jab tak jivan hai achchhe kam karo agli bar manav jivan kab milega pata nahi.
dusro ki madad karo ye mahan vyakti ke gun hai, kisi bhukhe ko khilao ye anmol karm hai.
jab ham bhagwan ke nam jap karte hai tab hamare under alag tarike ki urja mahsus hota.
nadi patthar ke bich rasta banati hai apne taqat ki vajah se nahi har na manne ki vajah se.
aapne jo laksh banaya hai use pane ke liye agar aap jaldi nahi karoge to samne dikhne vale laksh bhi bahut dur ho jayega. bade soch vichar karke kadam uthane chahiye
gyan hamare antar aatma me hai.
jo bhi aapko chahiye apne andar khojiye aapko sab kuch mil jayega bas dhyan lagaeye.
sukh aur dukh hame apne andar se hi hoti hai vo bahar se nahi hota ham apne manobhav se badal sakte hai khud ko khus rakh sakte hai.
duniya ko badne se pahle khud ke liye pahle khud ko badlana padta hai, aur ko badlne ke liye apne vichar ko badlana padta hai.
galti to har koi karta hai par us galti se milne vali shikh ko grahan karna chahiye, buddhiman purus dusro ke galti se sikhta hai.
