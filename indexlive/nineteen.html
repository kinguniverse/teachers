Anmol Vachan



manushy ko apne virudhy bate nahi karne chahiye isme svany ki hani hoti hai.
Apni ninda dvara vyakti prakarantar se apni prasansa karta hai, yesa karke vah keval yah dikhana chahta hai ki vah kitni chhut de sakta hai
Aptti ke liye dhan ki raksha karni chahiye, dhan se stree ki raksha karni chahiye, kintu dhan aur stree se sada apni raksha karni chahiye.
aatm sarkshan prakritik ka pahla niyam hai.
aatm vishvas me vah shakti hai jo sahstra vipatti ka samna kar un par vijay prapt karte hai.
aatm vishvas safalta ka pratham rahasy hai.
yah aatm vishvas rakho ki tum prithvi ke sabse aavashyak manushy ho.
aatm vishvas badane ki riti yah hai ki vah kam karo jisme tum karte huye darte ho, is prakar jese jese safalta milti jayegi aatm vishvas bhi badata jayega.
jis manushy me aatm vishvas nahi hai vah shaktiman hokar bhi kayar hai aur pandit hote bhi murkh hai
aatm vishvas ki kami hi hamare bahut sare asafalta ka karan hoti hai, shakti ke vishvas me shakti hai.
ve sabse kamjor hai, chahe ve kitne bhi shaktiman kyu na ho, jinhe apne aap tatha apni shakti par vishvas nahi hai.
aatm vishvas, aatm gyan aur aatm sanyam , keval vahi tin jivan ko param shakti sampanna bana deti hai.
aatm vishvas jaisa koi mitra nahi, aatm vishvas hi bhavi unnati ka pratham sidhi hai.
aatm vishvas ki kami hamari bahut si asafalta ka jad hai.
jo kamjor, prashrit aur hichkichane vala vyakti hai, ve aatm nirbhar rahne valo ki samanta nahi kar sakta.
jo aatm vishvas ham svany par rakhte hai uska adhikans bhag us vishvas se utpanna hota hai jo ham dusre pe rakhte hai.
