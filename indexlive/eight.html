Cement ka sipahi



Dukh sabhi ko hoti hai , kisi ko kam to kisi ko jyada, yesi hi ghatna hai.
hamari gaon ki ek privar ka .
us parivar me sirf do hi log the maa aur beta.
ve log behad grib the , garibi ke karan use padayi chhorna pada.
ve dono saty aur ahinsa ka pujari tha, imandari hi unka dhan dolat the.
beta ghar aaya :
maa ne puchha "kya huaa beta, aaj thora niras lag raha hai".
"kuch nahi maa paros ke gaon me ek cement ki fectory lagi hai, kal hi chalu huyi hai, mujhe kam karne jana hai maa, tabhi to me aur tu khus rah sakenge" itna kahkar vah chala gya.
subah huyi, maa ko pranam karke kam karne chala gya.
jab vah fectory pahucha to vaha ke malik ne puchha "kya nam hai tumhara".
"radheshyam".
"dekho radheshyam, hamare cement ki fectory hai, yadi tum yaha kam karna chahte ho to tum cement ki raksha karo , taki koi chori karkr na le ja paye".
yah sunkar radheshyam bahut khus ho gya.
sham ko ghar aakar maa ko gale laga liya aur kaha ki meri nokri pakki ho gyi, kal se me har roj kam pe jaunga.
"kya kam mila beta" maa ne puchha.
"cement ka sipahi" radheshyam ne kaha.
aur aage kahne laga , pata hai maa seth ji ne mera naam puchha uske bad puchha tumhari umra kitni hai , mene kaha 22 .
seth ji ne kaha ki tum abhi javan ho ye kam kar sakte ho aur maa pata hai har mahine mujhe 5 hajar rupay dega. br itna hi kahte mujhe sipahi bana diya.
maa me ab jake so jata hu , tum khana kha lena me aaj nahi khaunga.
maa ne kaha " beta mene khana kha liya hai, tu jake so ja".
radheshyam har roj kam pe jana laga aur apna kam pura sahas aur imandari se karne laga.
radheshyam ko achchhe insan ke rup me sabhi janne lage.

Aaj holi hai sabhi log holi khelne me mast hai , bhang sharab pikar sabhi madhos hai. pura gaon holi ki jasan manane laga.
sham ke vyakt holi khelkar radheshyam talab nahane ja raha tha tab usne dekha kuch gariya teji se aa raha hai, aaj to chhutti hai par ye gariya, radheshyam ko shak huaa.
radheshyam ne sari gariya rok liya aur uchi avaj me kaha "isme kya hai".
sabhi chup the, kuch der bad radheshyam ne fir kaha "kya hai isme aur kaha le ja rahe ho ".
fir kisi ne kuch nahi kaha. tab radheshyam khud gariyo me udh kar dekha to heran ho gya kyuki isme cement bhare huye the
"ye gariya kiski hai".
pichhe se ek aadmi ne kaha " ye hamre yaha ke sabse amir aadmi seth dhanshyam ki hai, thore der me seth ji aane vale hai ".
kuch der bad seth ji aaye aur kahne lage "kya huya beta, chhor do iske badle me aapko jo chahiye ham vahi denge ".
"mujhe kuch nahi chahiye aapne chori ki hai , aapko saja milegi."
"nahi beta yesa nahi karte me tumhe 10 hazar rupar deta hu aap chhor do".
thori der bad fir se seth ji ne kaha "beta dekho me tumhe 50 hazar rupay deta hu, hamari gariyo ko chhor do".
radheshyam ne jabab diya " dekho seth ji aap paiso ke amir hai aur me imandari ka , me apni imandari nahi bech sakta, kripya aap hamare saman vapas kar do".
seth dhanshyam jab har tarika apna kar thak gya tab apne nokar ko aadesh diya ki is sipahi ko khub maro .
seth ji ki aadesh pakar un sab ne radheshyam ko khub mara .
aur cement lekar bhag gya, radheshyam kisi tarah ghar aaya , maa use dekhkar rone lagti hai, ki ye kese ho gya .
fectory me halla ho gya ki chori huyi hai , fectory ke malik radheshyam ke pas aaya aur puchhne laga kya huaa tha. radheshyam ne sab sach sach kah diya, tab malik ne dhanshyam ko kes kiya aur apna sara saman vapas le aaya.
kuch din bad se radheshyam fir se kam pe jane laga. sabhi khusi se rahne lage .
