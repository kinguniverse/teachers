Bahadur Javan



Hasta tha, khilkhilata tha hamre sath, chehre me hamera muskan rahti.
ghar me ek bahan , maa aur vo tha, isliye sabhi usse pyar karte
dosto ke sath hamesa khus rahta , sapne bahut bade bade dekha karta tha.
jab me usse puchchhta tha "itne bade bade sapne kyu dekhta hai".
tab vah kahta "bade bade sapne dekhunga, tabhi to ek din bahut bada ban paunga".
naam tha amar , vo apne aap mo veer amar kahta rahta .
vo mera hi dost tha, bate bahut badi badi karta.
uske maa use bahut pyar karta tha kyunki uske pitaji nahi the ek choti bahan thi.

Andhera chhaya huya tha , subah ke karib 3:30 baj rahe the, thik se kuch dikhayi nahi de raha tha , tabhi mujhe ek aavaj sunayi di , me dar gya koi bhut pret to nahi, darte darte bahar nikla to dekha amar khada hai.
amar ne kaha "chalo dorne jayenge".
"kya hua aaj achanak".
"chal na aaj se dorte hai".
"olympic me jane ka plan hai kya".
"nahi desh ke liye kuch karna hai mujhe".
"kya karega".
"army bankar desh ki raksha karunga".
itni hi bate huye aur ham dono dorne chale gye.
pahla din tha , dono jaldi thak gye
mene kaha "chal ho gya".
"me mar jaunga fir bhi me army me jaunga".
uski jid hi kuch aur thi. unki bate me sachchayi thi, utsah tha.
uske har ek din dorne jate , me jaldi thak jata tha aur dorta hi rahta.
thora andhera tha , mene amar se kaha "chal koi kitna tej dor sakta hai".
dono tej dorne lage par amar ka per ek patthar se takra gya aur gir gya.
mere to hosh ur gye ki use kya huya, me bhagte huye uske pas pahucha dekha ghutne me thori chot lagi hai.
ghar aakar ham dono ne kuch nahi bataya.
batate to amar ko uski bahut gali deti.
me rat ko chen ki nind so gya kyuki subah dorne nahi jana tha kyuki use chot lagi thi.
me aaram se so gya.
jaise hi subah ke 3:30 baje , amar mere ghar chala aaya, yah dekh me heran rah gya.
mene kaha "aaj bhi dorega".
"chal dheere dheere dorenge".
vah rangrate rangrate dor fraha tha, mene socha ki vah aaj jaldi dhak jayega par nahi uski jid hi kuch alag thi.
me thak hara beth gya aur dekhta raha vo dor hi raha tha.
dopahar ka vyakt tha, me kahani likh raha tha, amar dorte huye mere pas aaya aur kaha " pata hai kal mera army ki dor hai".
itni hi bate huye aur vah chala gya. me uski confident dekhkar heran ho gya, kya gajab ka desh premi hai.
roj ki tarah me kahani likh raha tha, tabhi kisi ne mujhe kas kar pakda aur kaha "aaj me bahut khus hu ".
aavaj se pahchan liya ki amar hai.
amar ne kaha "me select ho gya" aur waha jo kuch kiya sab batane laga.
kuch din bad me aur amar ghar pe betha hua bate kar raha tha, tabhi dakiya aaya aur amar ko ek letter diya aur chala gya .
letter khol kar dekha to pata chala ki 10 tarik ko kashmir me joining karni thi.
amar ke chehre khil uthe.
vah dorte huye ghar gya aur maa aur bahan ko bataya dono khabar sunkar bahut khus huye.
amar ham sab ko chhor kar 10 tarik ko kashmir chala jata hai .
