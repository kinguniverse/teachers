Raja Devdutt ki jindagi



Bharat ki bhumi tyag aur tapasya ke bhumi hai .
bharat me mahatmayo ki kabhi kami nahi huyi
ek samay ki bat hai . bharat ke purv disha me mirdha nam ka ek suprasidhya sthan tha jiska raja suryvardhan tha .
suryvardhan ke 12 bete the jab suryvardhan ki mityu huyi tab rajygaddi ke liye sabhi bete me jhagra ho gya
Devdutt suryvardhan ka bada beta tha jisne apni 11 bhaiyo ko mar kar rajygaddi me betha .
ab Devdutt pure samrajy ka samrat ban gya .
usne apni samrajy ke dekhbhal ke liye kuch logo ka chayan kiya .

isi tarah kuch bars bit gaye

Ek din Devdutt ko pata chal kmi kanchanganjavali namak ek nagri hai jo bahut hi khubsurat aur hara bhara hai. aur vaha ke raja vikram bahut hi tagatbar hai. Devdutt ko dar lagne laga ki agar vikram hame aakraman kar de to hum barbad ho jayenge hamarin puri samrajy khatm ho jayegi. is dar ki bajah se Devdutt ne apni mantriyo ko bulakar failsa liya ki vikram se pahle hum us par aakraman kar uske samrajy par kabja kar lenge .
Aakram karne ki dusri bajah yah bhi thi ki vikram ke samrajy me behisab dhan dolat the , jisse Devdutt ko lobh bihar lag gya
Devdutt ne apni saina teyar karne lage ek nischay samay dekhakar ve apni bisal saina lekar aakraman karne nikal gaye
6 din gujarne ke bad ve vikram ke samrajy ke pas pahuche aur apni vishal saine ko ashtra dekar aakram karne ko aadesh diya .
idhar pure samrajy me hahakar fail gya ki dusman ne hamla kar diya , vikram ki saina bhi medan me aa gye .
Dono ke bich 12 din ghor yudh chala bahut se logo ke ang bhang ho gye , kisi ke hath kat gye , kisin ke per kat gye , kisi ke sir kat gye hajaro lakho log mare gye
kitne bachhe anath ho gye , kitne aurat vidhva ho gye , na jane kitne bahano ke bhai chale gye
ant me Devdutt ne vikram ki samrajy jit liya aur samrat vikram ko jail me dal diya .
ye sara khel dur khade ek sadhu dekh raha tha . is tarah ki tantav dekhkar bahut ghrina hui
Devdutt vapas aakar jit ke jasan manane laga , tarah tarah ke manoranjan sadhan aayojan karne laga
garibo ko dan dene lage pure samrajy me khusi ka mahol chha gya .
udhar koi rone laga to koi tarapne laga , har ek ghar me matam chha gya .
idhar sadhu vikram ki saina ki chikitsa karne laga , use davayi dene laga . sadhu ne niswarth logo ki seva karne laga jisse bahut logo thik hone lagne garibo aur vikram ki saina ke liye ye sadhu ek falista ban gya .
udhar kisi ne Devdutt ko suchna de diya koi sadhu dushman sainiko ki madad kar raha hai , ye sunkar Devdutt ko bahut gussa aaya aur apni saina lekar turant sadhu ke pas aa gya .
Devdutt kahne laga ki inki chikitsa mat karo tab sadhu ne kaha ki ye mera dharm hai . itna kahte hi Devdutt ne sadhu ko marne ki aadesh diya tab uske sainik ne sadhu ko bahut mara aur chetavani di ki agli bar se koi dushman ka chikitsa nahi karoge. itna kahkar chala gya

6 mahine bad

ek din samrat Devdutt sikar karne jangal me gye , ghanto bhar ghumne ke bad bhi koi sikar nahi mila , tab raja Devdutt ne aadesh diya ki jangal ke aur andar jaye . Devdutt teji se ja rahe the ki achanaK ghoro ko sap ne kat liya jaise hi sap ne kata ghora dhram se niche gir gya raja bhi gir gya , Devdutt ke muh se khun nikalne lage hath pero se khun tapakne lage , sainik ghabra gye ki ab kya kare .
sab sainik idhar udhar jane lage , ek sainik ne dekha ki koi aadmi nadi me shanan kar raha hai . sainik bhagte huye aadmi ke pas gya to dekha ki vo aadmi vahi sadhu tha jisse raja ke aadesh se mara tha.
sainik ne kisi tarah se sadhu ko manakar Devdutt ke pas le aaye . sadhu dekhkar heran ho gya khun jyada hi nikal chuka tha . sadhu apne aasharam gya aur kuch juri buti le aaye aur jaha jaha chot laga tha vaha laga diya . karib 10 miniute bad raja devdutt ko hos aaya , usne sadhu ko dekhkar ro diya aur kahne diya ki " hai mahatma mene aapko kitne dand diye fir bhi aapne mera itni amdad kiya ye kahakar raja rone laga ".
tab sadhu ne kaha dekho rajan aap mere sath aao aur kaha dekho ye chittia mar chuki hai kya tum ise jivan de sakte ho , devdutt ne kaha nahi tab sadhu ne bistar se samjhaya ki dekho rajan jab hum ek choti chiti ko bhi jivan nahi de sakte to hame kisi ki jindagi lene ka koi adhikar nahi hai aap yudh mat kijiye .
sadhu ki bat sunkar raja ne adesh diya ki jitne bhi sampatti jite hai vo sabhi usi ko dan de do jiski hai. aur samrat vikram ko aajat kar do aur use fir se samrat bana do ye meri aadesh hai .
tab se raja devdutt shanti ki jindagi lage .
sabhi santi se rahne lage.
