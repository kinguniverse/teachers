Badlav



Rahul nam ka ek officer tha jo engineering ki nokari karta tha.
achchha khasa paisa kama leta tha.
achchha chamkdar ghar dvar banaya log dekhte to vah vah karte.
ve kabhi bhi dyuti nahi chhorta ,hamesa upasthit rahta.
par unki aadat achchhi nahi thi, din dhalta gya, ab umra bhi bad gyi, nokri se retire ho gya.
retire hone ke bad ghar par hi rahne laga, par vaha ke log use bat chit nahi karte the kyuki rahul baki samay pas ke hotel me jakar juaa khelta.
sharab pite aur fijul kharcha karte. use vekar kamo me paisa udana ka thora bhi gam nahi tha.
Din bhar hotel me juaa khelta , sharab pita aur rat ko ghar aakar so jata.
uska na koi beta tha na patni.
ghar bahut ganda rahta kyuki saf safayi karne vala koi nahi tha .
subah uth kar muh dhote aur chale jate hotel aur rat ko aate.
Roj ki tarah vah ghar ke vahar kursi par bethkar brush kar raha tha tah usne dekha ki chhoti si bachchi jo karib 10 vars ka hoga.
vah ladki thik se chal nahi pa rahi thi mano uske per me koi bahut bari bimari ho.
yah dekhkar rahul ne puchha "bahan, kya huaa hai aapko jo aaapko chalne me dikkat ho rahi hai".
ladki ne kaha "ji, mere yah ek ghatna hai, jab me 5 vars ka tha, us din maa aur pita ji kam karne ke liye gye the aur me so rahi thi, achanak bhukamp aayi aur ek badi lakdi mere per me gir gyi mujhe bahut dard hone laga, kuch der bad maa babab aaye to ghabra gya, aur pitaji ne mujhe hospital le gya, doctor ne bataya ki opration karna hoga, jiske liye 20 hazar rupay lagega, ham log garib hai, itna paisa kaha se layenge, tyab se meri yesa halad hai ".
yah kah kar vo ladki chala gya.
rahul pura din ghar me hi betha raha ladki ki bate use bahut yad aa rahi thi, vo sochne laga ki mere pass itna pais hai aur me juaa khel kar paisa kharch kar deta hu, use apne aap me bahut bura laga.
sochne laga ki "na jane kitne yese bachhe hai jiski yesi halad huya hai" rahul ne faisala kiya ki aaj se daru, juaa bad ab se achchhe kam.
agle hi din :
rahul ladki ke ghar aa gya aur uske mata pita se kaha ki ab se aapki beti fir se achchhe se chal payegi, opration ka sara paisa me dung.
yah sunkar ladki ke mata pita bhavuk ho gye aur duaa dene lage aur kahne laga "aap hamare ghar me fariste ki karah aaye ho ".
rahul ek bar fir hotel gya par is bar na juaa khela na sharab piya , vaha hone vali sor use ab sahan nahi ho rahi thi, mano dam ghut raha ho.
rahul ab apne sharabi dosto ko bhi samjhane laga ki dekho sharab pine aur juaa khelne se kuch nahi hoga, jab tak ham kisi ki madad na kare apne dosto ko kisi tarah samjhakar sab ne faisla liya ki aas pas ke gaon me jitne bhi yese bachhe hai ham un sabhi bachho ka opration aur dvayi ka kharcha uthayenge.
rahul ki ye kam dekhkar aas pas me use neta ki tarah samman dene lage.
dheere dheere ye kam ek fundastion ban gya. jo insano ko free me ilaj karne lage. sarkar bhi iske ye kam ke liye support karne lage.
ab aas pas ke amir log yaha aakar kuch paisa dan karte taki jaruratmando ki madad ki jaye. garibo ki seva ek mahan kam hai.

subah ka samay tha log bagiche me bedhkar bate kar rahe the, tabhi ek vyakti ne jor se aavaj di . sabhi uske pas gye.
tab us vyakti ne dheere se kaha ki "rahul, hamare neta ab duniya me nahi rahe".
yah sunkar matam ka mosam chha gya, sabhi ek dusre ke kanthe pe kantha rakhkar rone laga.
nam aankho se rahul ko antim bitayi di, uski ek badi si tasvir banayi aur mahan aatma ke rup me use sab pujne lage.
ek chhoti si ghatna jindagi badal deti hai.
aaj rahul hamre sath nahi hai to kya huaa uski yade to sath hai.
jab bhi uske bare me bat karta hu to pata hi nahi chalta ki kitna time bit chuka.
