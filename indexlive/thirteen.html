Maa Ka Shikh



Jade ka din tha, barsat ho rahi thi, darvaje par kisi ki aane ki khabar sunayi di jakar darvaja khula to dekha pitaji hai
maa kuchh kam kar rahi thi to mene hi chay banayi.
barish me pitaji bhig gye the.
me mobile dekh raha tha, tab pitaji ne kaha ki "6 mahine bad 10th ka exam hai padayi kar".
rat ho rahi thi barsat ke karan bijli nahi thi.
mene lamp jalaya aur padne beth gya karib ek ghanta padne huye the tab ek dost ne pukara , me padayi chhor uske sath chala gya.
kuch der bad maa meri kamre me aaye aur dekha me vaha nahi hu, jab me ghar aaya tab maa ne kaha "kaha gya tha ja khana laga hai kha le aur padne beth".
me khana khane chala gya , khana khaya aur so gya .
agle din nahaya khana khaya aur school chala gya, school se vapas aaya aur khelne chala gya uske bad rat ko padayi ki aur so gya.
me jyadatar cricket khelte hi bitata.
jo mere ghar valo ko pasand nahi thi, pata nahi ek din mujhe kaya huaa mujhme khelne ki jo khusi thi vo nahi rahi.
ab mujhe khelne ka man hi nahi karta.
me thik se padne laga maa bhi bahut khus ho gyi aur hum sab achchhe se rahne lage.
hum vah nahi jo ham dikhte hai, hum vo hai jo ham sochte hai.
aaj kal manusy kyu hinsa karta hai, aaj log khud upar uthne ke liye dusre logo ka balidan deta hai.
apne aap ki huye tarif logo ko thik nahi lagti, agar aap khud ko shresth samjhte ho to khud ki tarif mat karo, chup raho.
mitha bolo isse charo aur sukh badta hai aur log priyata badti hai, kathor vachan ka tyag karna hi sabse bada vashikaran hai.
kisi ko mat maro jab ham kisi ko marte hai to hum apni hi shreshta ko khatam kar deti hai.
yadi tum chahte ho ki log tumhari vastvik yogyta se jyada samjhe to tum kab bolo, buddhiman ki bat karne ki apeksha buddhiman pratit hona achchha hai.
muh se yesi bani bolni chahiye jisse sunne valo ki dil me thanthak ho.
ek bat ko anek shabdo me mat kaho balki ek hi shabad me anek bate kah do.
shiksha ka kam paisa kamana nahi balki chritya ka nirman karna hai.
samay par sadbuddhi aa jaye to isse badi khusi aur kuchh nahi.
kam bolo aur aage badte jao adhik bolkar khud ki yogta nast na karo.
jo puny karta hai uska vivek buddhi badta hai jo jiska vivek buddi ho vo sada punay hi karta hai, jo pap karta hai uska vivek buddhi kam ho jata hai aur jiska vivek buddi kam ho vo sada pap hi karta hai.
mahapandit vishnugupta chankya ne kaha tha "hamare vivek buddhi karm ke anusar badti hai jo jaisa karm karta hai use vesa hi vivek buddi hoti hai isliye manvo ko yah salah di jati hai ki vo sada achhe karm kare".
is prithvi me sukh dukh dono aate hai. ye sab isvar ka khel hai.
sirf hame ek achchhi soch sochni chahiye uske bad dekhna ishvar hame yese upar uthayegi ki hame bhi purn vishvas nahi hoga.
juaa khelne se do mahan chich ki nas hoti hai pahla dhan aur dusra samay
is maya rupi duniya ko par karne ka ekmatra sadhan hai bhakti, bhakti se bada aur kuch nahi.
gyan se ham bhagwan ko nahi pa sakte bhakti hi ekmatra rashta hai.
man ke hare har hai aur man ke jite jit isliye man me hi kabhi nahi harna.
manushya ki aabsyakta ki purti ho sakti hai par lalsa ki nahi.
sansar me manav koi shreni ke hai, kuch apne aur apne parivar tak simit hai, iske prayas sirf apne sukh suvidha ke liye hai.
shreshth log apne aur apnepan se upper uthkar bramand ka kalyan karte hai.
kuch bhi karne se pahle ghambhirta se soch lo , apne aap ki aavaj suno, dusre kya kahte hai uska aaklan mat karo, laksh nirdharit kar aur kary aarambh kar.
apne bare me bada chda kar mat kahna kyuki aapki falti bate aapko ek din bahut kasht degi.
