Me kyu likhta hu



Me kyu likhta, ye ek kathin saval hai par hai bahut rochak, har ek insan ko mohit kar deta hai.
is prasn ka javab dena to aasan nahi hai, me khud ko samjhne ke liye likhta hu kyuki me jis tarah sochta hu usi tarah ki kalpana kar sakta hu aur me jis tarah ke ki kalapana kar sakta hu usi tarah kahani likh sakta hu.
me kahani likh kar apne swabhav ko jan sakta hu, apni aatma ko jan sakta hu ki kya vo hame karne ke liye bol raha hai.
apne andar ke swarup ko jan pata hu.
me kahani nahi likhta hu , balki apne dil me jo hai use prakat karta hu.
jo jaisa kahani likhta hai, usse ye pata chalta hai ki vo insan apne me kya sochta hai, vah kis tarah ka insan hai , vo swabhav kesa hai.
me tab kahani likh pata hu jab man sant hota hai, sharir aur dil kahta hai ki ab likh. sharir apne aap ek alag sanket deti hai
kahani antaratma se suru hota hai, jab tak anubhuti ki trivata na ho tab tak kahani likhna bahut hi kathin hai.
kahani likhne ka matlab apna vichar vyakt karna hai, apne vichar ko duniya ke samne rakhana hai.
hamare prithvi me bahut yese mahan purus huye jo apni bhavana ko vyakt karke duniya me mahan ban gye.
dorna hai to junun se doro, khelna hai to junun se khelo, padna hai to junun se pado, likhna hai to junun se likho. kuch bhi karna hai junun se karo
kahani matlab ek ghatna, jo ham man hi sochte hai aur use kagaj par likhte hai usi me apni bhavana vyakt karte hai, usi me apni sari taqat dal deti hai.
kahanikar apne dil me hi ek ghatna banati hai, use soch vichar kar sajati hai, uske bad kagaj par likhakar kahani ka rup de deti hai.
jo bhi kahani likhni hoti hai use suddh rup se likho taki dusre aadmi kahani ko samajh sake.
aap jitni kahaniya padoge, aap utni hi kahani aur likhne ke liye prarit hoge.
kahani ka ek mahatvpurn ans chhat kar, unka shirsak banaye.
Jo sarvshresth kahanikar hote hai kabhi bhi nakartmak chhabi kahani me nahi banata balki kahani ke bahane apni dil ki bate likhte hai.
bahut se kahanikaro ne sarkar ki aankhe khol hai, kahani ke bahane logo ki pristhitiya,. uski kathinayi sarkar ke samne rakhi aur sarkar so us samsyayo ka samathan karne ke liye aagrah kiya.
kuch kahani kar kathinayi kese samadhan hogi vo bhi likh dete haijisse sarkar ko sahayata milti hai kathin faisla lene me.
hum apni kahani me jitna kho jayenge utna hi achchhi achchhi kahaniya likh payenge.
kahanikar har ek jagah, har ek pristhitiya me kahani khoj leta hai jaise koi train me safar karte karte kahani likh deta hai.
to koi band darvaje ke andar bethkar kahani likh deta hai.
likhne se aatm bal badta hai aur aatmbal badne se hamari kam karni ki shakti viksit hote hai.
kahani man ka nahi, dil ka bhav hai.
me chahtu hu ki har ek insan kahani likhe aur kahani ko yese likhe jaise ki padne valo ki usme kho jayer us kahani me vo khud ko mahsus kare.
kahni ek rangin sapna hai, hum aankhe khol kar likhte huye sapne dekhte hai.
kitne ghante bethe rahte hai likhte likhte par kabhi yesa nahi laga ki me thak gya hu, jab meri soch khatam ho jati hai.
tab me fir sochta hu ki ab kya sochu aur kya likhu, sochte sochte dil me hi ek kahani ban jati hai jise kahani ke rup me likh deta hu.
har ek kahani kar ek bar yah jarur likhta hai ki me kyu likta hu.
