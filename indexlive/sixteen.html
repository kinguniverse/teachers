Manushy Jivan



Manushy ko Sadachar aur propkar purn jivan jina chahiye.
sadachar ka arth hai achchha aacharan aur propkar ka arth hai dusro ka upkar.
Kisi ne thik kaha hai agar tum samman chahte ho to aapko bhi dusro ko samman dena hoga.
jivan me bahut yese pal aate hai jab halad bahut dald se bhari ho aur samjh me na aaye kya kare tab kisi ekant jagah me jakar apne dil se puchna ki kya kare vo bata dega.
mujhe jab bhi presaniya aati hai tab me shant rahkar khud ko hi saval karta hu, aur presaniya ka samadhan ho jata hai.
bipatti aane par shant rahna mahan aatma ka gun hai.
karmo ki dhavni sabhi dhavni se uchhi hoti hai.
manushy ko kathor vani ko ka tyag karna chahiye, madhur vani charo aur sukh samridhi badata hai aur sunne vala bhi aanandit hota hai.
isse log priyta badta hai aur samman char guna ho jata hai.
aaj aaye aur kal chale jayenge hai manav es bich jo samay milta hai usak sad upyog kar lo.
har insan jina chahta hai, marna koi nahi chahta har insan ko apna jivan priy hai, na kuch lekar aaye na kuch lekar jayenge jo hai yahi hai, jo aaj tumhara hai kal kisi aur ka tha parso kisi aur ka hoga jise tum apna samajh kar magan ho raho ho vo sirf ek maya jal hai .
manushya apne jivan ki muly ko kyu nahi samjhta yah jivan anmol hai, is jivan ka sahi upyog tabhi hoga jab iska fal kisi aur ko mile, jab hum nisvarth madad karna shik jayenge tab log hame uche pad ki upadhi degi.
manushy kesa hai kesi sthiti me hai isse ye darsata hai ki usne aaj tak kya socha hai.
jiska jaisa savbhav hota hai vo vesa hi rahta hai kyu savbhav bahut kam logo ka badlta hai.
us aachran ko, us dhan ko, us patni ko tyag dene me hi bhalayi hai jo hamare kul ki yogta ko nast karta ho.
saty kathor hota hai par saty se bada aur kuch nahi hota.
kamjor insan saty ko sah nahi pata, saty ko sahne ke liye bahut taqat ki jarurat hoti hai.
aaj sab log padna janta hai par kya padna chahiye koi nahi janta aaj paiso ke liye padte hai samaj kalyan ke liye koi nahi padta.
aaj tak kisi amir insan ko mahan nahi kaha gya , mahan paiso se nahi banta , mahan tyag aur tapsya ka rup hai pyar ka rup hai.
hamare andar rahne vale aatma hi parmatma hai, kahte hai na ki ishvar ko kaha khoj rahe ho vo to tumhare andar hi hai, apne man mandir me jakar dekho khud bhagwan aapka intjar kar raha hai ki kab vo apne ko dekhega, kab dil ki dharkan ki ghanti bajakar aayega.
jis tarah ham kisa ka injar karte hai usi tarah bhagwan bhi hamara injar karta hai par hum upper dekhkar kahte hai, vaha bhagwan hai, hum apne aap ko kabhi nahi dekhte.
bhagwan hamse alag nahi hai sab insan bhagwan ka hi ansh hai.
hamsab chand paiso ke liye itni badi saltanat ko kho de rahe hai, ek yesi duniya hai jaha hamara bhi koi intjar kar raha hai.
hum is sharir me hi magan ho jate hai iske under virajman ishvar hame najar nahi aata.
har jagah bhagwan hai, har ek chich me bhagwan hai, hamari soch bhi pahle se likhi huyi hai, hum kathaputli hai jo idhar udhar bhag rahe hai, aur koi aaram se bedhkar dekh raha hai aur has raha hai.
insano ki ghamand ko dekhkar, uske dusre ke prati aachran ko dekhkar ham khel rahe hai aur umpire kahi aur .bethkar maja le raha hai.
bhagwan hai , chahe ham kitna bhi pad le kitni badi pandin ban jaye bhir bhi bhagwan nahi milega.
bhagwan ko gyan se nahi bhakti se paya jata hai.
jitne bhi mahan puruso ne bhagwan ko paya hai sabne bhakti se hi paya hai. pyar me bahut taqat hoti hai. bhagwan ko bhi majbur bana deta hai.
