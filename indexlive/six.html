Badi Maa



Ek 5 vars ka ladka apne pitaji ke god me bethkar kahne laga, "pitaji , jab me bada ho jaunga na , tab me aapko tajmahal dikhane le jaunga me vaha jakar khilona kharidunga pitaji".
"aur apni maa ko le nahi jaoge".
"le jaunga".
"aur apni badi maa ko".
"nahi le jaunga".
"kyu beta".
"vah mujhe pyar nahi karti , mujhe datdi rahtib hai".
uske pitaji ne do shadi ki thi, pahli patni ki bachchhe nahi huyeisliye uske pitaji ne dusri shadi kiya.
isliye vo ladka apne ma ko maa kahta aur use badi maa.
uski badi maa ki koi santan nahi thi isliye vo isse jalti thi.

Garmi ka din tha , sabhi ke sharir se pasina nikal raha tha . itne me vo ladka dorta hua aaya aur kahne laga "mujhe bhukh lagi hai ,khana do".
tab uski badi maa ne kaha "bhagwan ne tujhe hath aur per diye hai na , khan le le, kha kha kar sand ki tarah hota ja raha hai ".
yah sunakar vo bachchha rone laga, rone ki aavaj sunakar unki maa dorti huyi aayiaur kahne lagi "Are mere sona beta kya hua, ro kyu raha hai , achchhe bachchhe rote nahi beta".
apni maa ki aadar aur pyar pakar vah chup ho gya aur kahne laga "maa mujhe bhukh lagi hai".
"ha beta me tumhe apni hatho se khilaunga". yah dekha kar aur bhi jalti thi.
dopahar ka vyakt tha uske pitaji kheti ka kam karke aaye aur kahne lage "are bhagwan, pyas lagi hai pani do, jaldi pani lao".
uski badi maa ne pani la di , pani pine ka bad puchha " raju kaha hai".
itne me vo ladka dorte huye aaya aur kahne laga "pitaji me yahi pe hu".
uske pitaji ne use kandhe par utha liya. vah bachha bahut khus hua aur kahne laga "me , maa aur pitaji se bhi bada ho gya, ye kahkar hasne laga".
ye hasi unke badi maa ko sahan nahi hueaur kahne lagi "itna pyar achchha nahi hai, jab dekho god me hi chipka kar rakhta hai aur man hi man gali dene laga."
tab uske pitaji ne kaha "tum ise hamesha datdi kyu ho, yah tuhara hi to beta hai, isse tum pyar se bat kyu nahi karti".
yah sunkar vo muh fulakar chali gayi.
vah pitaji ke sath khelne lage kuch der bad uske maa aayi. aur raju maa ke god me bedhkar jid karne laga ki " maa kahani sunao, maa kahani sunao".
bachhe ki jid ke samne har ek maa har jati hai aur kahani sunane lagi , yah dekhakar badi maa andar hi andar jalne lagti. man hi man gali deti ki " tu marta kyu nahi, tu marega to mujhe shanti milengi,iski vajah se koi mujhe puchta bhi nahi"
ek vars bad :
badi maa ghar ke kone me bethi thi, raju dorta huaa aaya aur kahane laga "badi maa khelte khelte hath me chot lag gayi, khun nikal raha hai ,badi maa jaldi davayi la do na" .
"tebal par davayi rakha hai le le ".
yah kah kar badi maa dusre kamre me chali gyi, idhar raju ka khun nikal raha tha, raju kad me chhota tha isliye table rakhe davayi tak nahi pahuch pa raha tha, khun nikal hi raha tha .
kuch der bad uski maa aayi aur dekhi to dar gyi , khun dekhkar rone lagi aur kahne lagi "hai ram , ye kya o gya beta ".
jaldi jaldi davayi laga di aur maa ke god me so gya , raju bahut khus ho gya kyuki vo maa ke aanchal me so gya, isse svarg se bhi jyada khusi mili
na kisi ka dar na kisi ka chinta.
raju ek do din me thik ho gya. do din bad
raju aur pitaji khel rahe the, tab raju ne kaha me aapke itna bada banna chahta hu. raju ko table pe khada kar diya aur kisi kam se bahar chala gya tabhi unki badi maa aayi, raju jid karne laga ki mujhe niche utaro. niche utar hi rahi thi ki kisi tarah hath fisal gya aur raju gir gya.
raju ko girte dekh badi maa behosh ho gyi, 1b din bad hosh aaya aur puchhne laga "raju kesa hai ".
tabhi raju aa gya , raju ko dekh uski badi maa bhavuk ho gyi aur kahne lagi "beta, mujhe maf kar dena , me tumhe girne se bacha sakti thi, beta raju, ab me tumhe kabhi nahi datunga, tumse pyar karunga beta, tu mere kokh se na janma ho to kya huya tune to mujhe maa kahi hai."
aaj se me khana khilaunga, kahani sunaunga
kuch der raju socha aur fir kahne laha " aapko bhi me tajmal dekhne le jaunga".
yah sunkar sab hasne lage.
