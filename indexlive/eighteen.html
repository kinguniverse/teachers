Jo Log Kahte the



Purano ke anusar svarg vo sthan hai janha devta rahte hai aur achchhe karm karne vale insan ki aatma vaha sthan milta hai.
iske biprit bure karm karne valo ko nark me bheja jata hai.
Bhagwat geeta ke anusar kam, krodh,aur lobh ye nark ke tin darvaje hai.
jo log prem se jivan jita hai uske liye yahi duniya savarg hai aur jo bure karm karte hai uske liye yahi nark hai.
sabh jante hai na kuch lekar aaye the, naa kuch lekar jayenge khali hath aaye aur khali hath chale par is mahan saty ko janne ke bad bhi log kam, vashna ko tyag nahi kar pate aur jivan chakra me ghumte rahte hai.
apno ko dukhi karke kon khus raha hai, jab logo ko musibat aa jata ha tab punjanam ko dos dekar khud ki galti par parda dalta hai.
agar aap achchhe karm karte ho to der hi sahi aapko aapka fal avasy milega sirf dhery rakho aur shanti se karm karte jao.
apne jindgi ko pahchano, kartavy ko samjho, saty ke raste par chalo, asaty ko tyag do.
is duniya me jine ke liye prem aur dhan dono ki jarurat hoti hai isliye dhan ki rakshya karni chahiye bure vaykt me kam aati hai.
har insan ke andar chhupi huyi dard avasy hoti aur har insan me apna kuch gun hota hai aur vo insan usi me magn hokar apna jivan bita deta hai.
saty ke raste me chaloge to giroge haroge par ant me jit aapki hi hogi ye atal saty hai.
gun apna aadar kara leta hai, guni purus krod nahi karte krod se uske gyan nas ho chate hai, vah shant rahkar gyan ko aur badate hai.
kam bolne se hamare andar ki shakti badti hai.
jab tak ichchha rahit bhakti na ho tab tak ishvar ko pane muskil hai.
is prithvi me karm ko hi mahtv diya hai.
ham manav jati ke hone ke karan hamara kartvy hai ki sabhi jivo par dya rakhe. dya hi muly hai.
jaha ghamand hai vaha krodh hai vaha hazaro badgun hai hame apne dil ko pvitra karana chahiye.
Agar aap achchhe kam karte ho to musibat ke samay hazaro hath kade rahenge, agar aap dusro ko dukh dete ho to aapko musibat ke samay koi sahayta nahi karega.
garib, niakshar , agyan ka seva karna hi ishvar ka puja karne jaisa hai.
agar aap saty ke path par chalte ho aur koi musibat aa jata hai to aap musibat se ladna mat saty ke path par chalte rahna musibat apne aap mit jayega, bas dhery rakho. agar me musibat se ladne laga to me usi me fas kar rah jaunga
aatam raksha ke liye marne se jyada marne marne ki himmat honi chahiye.
uttam gyan aur satvichar kabhi katam nahi hota.
is duniya ka sabse bad dharm hai bhukhe ko khana khilana.
hamara buddi hamare karm ke anusar aage badne vali hai.
hame vo kam karcha chahiye jo sahi ho na ki aasan, bina mange salah nahi deni chahiye.
hamara kam vo hona chahiye jisme hame achchha lage. aapka dil hi aapka rasta dikhata hai.
dusro ki madad karna sikho ye shresth gun hai.
mon aur ekand aatma ko do sabse achchhe mitra hai.
chhoti chhoti bato par bichlit nahi honi chahiye jo aapko laksh ko bhatka de yesi vichar ko apne dil se nikal dene me hi bhalayi hai.
jo bit jata hai use bhul jane me hi bhalayi hai kyuki bite huye kal kitne hi achchhe kyu na ho vo hame dukh hi deti hai.
bhavishy kabhi nahi hota jo hai vo aaj hi hai isliye aaj ko ji lo kal kabhi nahi aata.
laksh ko jivan samjho, unka chintan karo uske sapne dekho kyunki laksh bhagwan ke aadesh ke atirikt aur kuch nahi hai.
logo ko samman do aapko vapas samman milega ye saty hai.
kam bolna aur soch samajh kar bolna mahan puruso ka gun hai.
log bahut kuch kahenge unki bato ko dil se lekar, laksh se mat bhatko unki bato ko chhoro aur laksh ke raste par chalte raho.
