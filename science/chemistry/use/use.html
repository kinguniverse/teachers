Organic Compounds and their uses









Methane ?

Use

Black Color, used in making motor type and printing ink , in the production of light and energy , in making methyl alcohol , formaldehyde and chloroform etc

Formic Acid

Use

in making insecticide, as a preservator of fruits juices , in trade of leather , rubber etc

Urea

Use

in the form fertilizer, in making formaldehyde ,urea , plastic etc

in making barnish, cardite, clodian celulose, artificial silk, synthetic rubber , sulphone , chlorotone, chloroform, iodoform etc as medicines

Use

chloroform

Use

in surgical operation as an anaesthesia, in form of solvent of rubber, fat ,lac etc, as an insecticide etc

Glucose

Use

in making different type of wine, in sweets and preservetors of fruits juices, medicines like gluconate

Phenol

Use

in the production of carbolic soap, in the form of insecticide, in bakelite, in phenestine, aspirin, celolal etc

Butane

Use

in liquid state it is used as LPG fuel

Ethylene

Use

in fruit ripening and fruits preservetor, in making mustard gas, in the form of n anesthesia, in producing oxy-ethylene flame etc

Polythene

Use

in producing electrical resistance in wires and cables, in making layer in the cap of the bottles, pipes, buckets etc

Ethyl alcohol ?

Use

in making wine and other alcoholic drinking

Methyl alcohol ?

Use

in making methyleted spirit , artificial colour, barnish and polish, mixing with petrol and used as a fuel of engines etc

Benzene ?

Use

in the form of a solvent, in dry cleaning, by mixing it with petrol and used as a fuel of engines etc

Acetamide

Use

in softening leather , cloth and in moisting pulp and paper

Formamint

Use

in making medicine of throat in making chewing tables etc

Acetic acid ?

Use

As a laboratory's reagent, in the form of vinegar, in making sauces and jelly etc

Ethyl Acetate ?

Use

in making madicines, artificial perfumes etc

formaldehyde

Use

in making insecticides, in fixation of giletin film on the photographic plates, in making waterproof cloths by mixing it with eggs exterior whitey part etc

Acetylene ?

Use

in producing light, oxy-ethylene flame, in the form of narselin anaesthesia, in making neoprine (artificial rubbe), in artificial fruits ripening etc

Polystrene

Use

in the production of caps bottles of acid, in making the body of the accumulator cells etc

Ethyl bromide ?

Use

in making local anaesthesia

Acetyl chloride ?

Use

in making acetamide, acetic anhydride etc

glyceral

Use

in making nitroglycerine, in clening the component parts of watches, in the ink of stamp, in shoes polish and cosmetics, in transparent soaps, in pain reliever medicines of the fractured parts of the body (organs) , in sweets, wine and as a fruits preservator etc

Acetic anhydride ?

Use

in colour industry, in the manufacturing of medicine like aspirin , in making artificial (synthetic) silk from cellulose , etc

Oxalic acid

Use

in colouration and printing of cloths, in making colour of ink and coatar, in the bleaching of leather, in cleaning the spot of ink by 10% solution etc
