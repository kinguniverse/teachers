Mineral wealth of Jharkhand (Important Notes)



Copper


Copper is a physical element, its sign is Cu, its atomic number is 29 and atomic weight is 63.5, is a ductile metal which is used as a conductor of electricity.
Copper is found in Ghatshila, Rakha, Musabani, Basidih, Dhobani, Seraikela, Kharsawan and Baharagora of Jharkhand and Jharkhand comes third in India.

asbestos


Mica is a versatile mineral that is found in the form of fragments in igneous and metamorphic rocks. It can be cut in very thin layers. It is colorless or light yellow, green or black in color. Sometimes the silicate mineral also turns into mica by being affected by wind and sunlight etc.
It is found in Koderma, Hazaribagh, Giridih, Jhumri Tilaiya, Dhorkola Damchanch in Jharkhand and Jharkhand comes in fourth place in India from the point of view of abrak.

Iron ore


Iron ores are those rocks and minerals from which metallic iron can be economically extracted. These ores usually contain high amounts of iron (iron or iron) oxides and can range in color from dark gray to bright yellow, deep purple, and rusty red.
It is found in Singhbhum, Daltonganj and Dhanbad in Jharkhand, Jharkhand comes third in India from the point of view of iron ore.

bauxite


Bauxite is an ore of aluminum. It is the main source of aluminum in the world. ... It is found in the laterite rocks of the Tertiary period.
It is found in Lohardaga, Ranchi, Palamu and Jadugoda of Jharkhand and comes third in Jharkhand, India from the point of view of bauxite.

Uranium


Uranium is a shiny white metal. Its sign is U, atomic number 92, atomic weight 238.03, melting point 1,130°C, boiling point estimated at 3,500°C, density 19.05 grams per cubic cm, electrical resistivity 32.76×109 ohm cm and crystal structure at three sides, at room temperature. Uranium is the active element.
Uranium is found in Jadugoda in Jharkhand and it comes first in India.

cryonite


Kryonite is found in Jharkhand's Raj Kharsawan, Lapsaburu, Singhbhum and it comes in the first place in India.

graphite


Graphite is a polymorph of carbon, it has a special kind of luster and it is a good conductor of electricity and heat. Its relative density is 2.25.
Graphite is found in Sakra mine and Daltonganj of Jharkhand.

manganese


Manganese is a chemical element that is chemically a member of the transition metals group. It is not found in its pure form in nature, but in compounds formed with other elements, which often include compounds of iron. After purification, its color is gray and if it is mixed with steel, the steel does not rust.
Manganese is found in Gua, Noamundi, Jamda and Chaibasa of Jharkhand and it comes in seventh place in India.

Coal


Coal is a solid organic material that is used as a fuel. Coal is very important as a major source of energy. Coal accounts for 35% to 40% of the total energy used. Among other sources of energy, the name of petroleum and its products is paramount.
Coal is found in Jharkhand's Jharia, Chandrapura, Bokaro, Ramgarh, Karna Pura, (Damodar Valley area), Giridih, Daltonganj, Hutar (North Koel Valley Coalfield) and it comes in the first place in India.

limestone


Limestone is a sedimentary rock composed mainly of various crystalline forms of calcium carbonate (CaCO3) such as the minerals calcite and/or aragonite. ... Limestone is available in many varieties. It is of various qualities in colour, configuration, hardness and durability.
It is found in Damodar Valley, Santhal Parganas, Ranchi and Singhbhum of Jharkhand.

tin


It is found in Ranchi and Hazaribagh of Jharkhand.

ceramic


Ceramics are used in making utensils, cups, bowls, plates, hospital articles, electrical insulators, spark plugs of motor vehicles, heat-resistant bricks, etc. Ceramic is used as a supplement in the manufacture of rubber, cloth and paper. Sometimes it is also fed as medicine.
It is found in Palamu, Ranchi and Singhbhum of Jharkhand.

Chromium


Chromium is a chemical element that is chemically a member of the transition metals group. It is a lustrous, grey-brown, hard but easily broken metal. When it is polished, it becomes very shiny and it does not smudge easily. Its melting temperature is 1907 °C, which is very high.
It is found in Jonhatu, Seraikela and Kuru of Jharkhand.
