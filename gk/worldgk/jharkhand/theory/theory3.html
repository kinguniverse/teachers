Waterfall (Important Notes)



Hundru Falls


This waterfall is located in the Swarnrekha river, it is the second highest waterfall of Jharkhand, it falls from a height of about 320 feet, it is located about 28 km from Ranchi.

Jonha Falls


This waterfall is situated in the Radhu river, it is also called Gautamdhara. It is said that Lord Buddha had bathed here, after which a temple of Buddha was built, it falls about 140 feet in height, it is about 40 km from Ranchi. and is 39 kms from Ranchi Railway Station

Dasam Falls


It is situated in the Kanchi river, consists of ten rivers, hence the name Dasam, it falls from a height of about 130 feet, it is near Tomar village on the Tata-Ranchi road, it is 36 km from Ranchi.

Budha Consumed Falls


This waterfall is located on the Budha river, so this waterfall is called Budha Falls, it is the highest waterfall in the state, it falls from a height of about 450 feet, it is located about 15 km from Mahuadand

hiranee jalaprapaat


This waterfall is situated on the Garha river, it is located on the Ranchi-Chaibasa road in Paschim Singhbhum district, it is situated in the middle of the forests and is famous for its natural beauty.

Motijhara Falls


This waterfall is situated on the Ajay river, its height is about 150 feet, it is located 3 km south of Maharajpur railway station.

Rajrappa Falls


This waterfall is situated at the confluence of Damodar river and Bheda river, its height is about 15 feet, it is situated 25 km east of Ramgarh.

Ghaghri Falls


This waterfall is located in the Ghaghari river, its height is about 140 feet and it is located 7 km north of Netarhat in Latehar district.

Sadani Consummate Falls


This waterfall is located in the river Shankh, its height is about 200 feet, it is located in Gumla district.
