Importaint Notes









Jharkhand is the most mineral rich state of India

The Damodar Valley region of Jharkhand is called the warehouse of minerals.

Jharkhand ranks first in coal, copper and mica production

Iron is obtained mostly from hematite ore in Jharkhand.

In Jharkhand, 33.85 percent of the country's total production of copper is produced.

Indian Kupper Complex has been doing the work of copper extraction since 1924.

Lead is mainly obtained from Palamu, Hazaribagh and Dumka.

Here 46.5 percent mica of the whole country is produced.

Jharkhand ranks first in the world in the storage of cronite.

Graphide is found in Palamu district, it is also called black lead.

Jharkhand ranks first in India in the production of apatite.

About 36 percent of the total mineral reserves of India are found in Jharkhand.

Jharkhand produces 32.28 percent of the total coal of the country.

A uranium factory has been set up at Jadugoda.

Tundu lead refining factory has been established near Dhanbad
