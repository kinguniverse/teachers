Importaint Notes









Jharkhand was established on 15th November as the 28th state of India.
The length of the state from north to south is 380 kilometers and breadth from east to west is 463 kilometers, its total area is 79714 square kilometers.
This province is world famous for its mineral wealth.
Jharkhand is formed from the oldest rocks of Archean era, in which granite is predominant.

Its north western part is relatively high, it is called Pat region.
The highest peak of Jharkhand is Parasnath, whose height is 1365 meters.
Due to the abundance of mineral resources in Jharkhand, it is also called the Rur state of India.
Agriculture is the mainstay of the economy of Jharkhand.
Jharkhand comes in tropical climate zone, The climate here is monsoon and it receives about 140 cm of rainfall. and tropical monsoon deciduous forests are found here
There are three types of weather: 1 summer (March-June), 2 rainy (June-October), winter (November-February) and the average temperature here is 25 degree centigrade.
Palamu is the hottest and Netarhat is the coldest state in Jharkhand. Most of the rainfall here comes from the south-west monsoon and the average rainfall here is 140 cm.
In December-January, there is little rainfall from the Norwester storm that rises from the Bay of Bengal, which is also called Kaal Vaisakhi or Amr Varsha and it also receives rain from the monsoon branch of the Arabian Sea.


We were taught in childhood that man is made of five elements, one of which is soil, so now we will read about soil, Let's see what is our soil like



Balthar soil - This soil is mostly found near the Rajmahal range, it is red colored, acidic, yellowish and pebbled, it is a fertilizer.
Red soil - This soil is mostly found in Chotanagpur plateau, East and West Singhbhum, Giridih. This soil is red in color, iron-rich, nitrogen, phosphorus and hum-deficient and pebbles.
Laterite soil - This soil is found maximum in Ranchi, Palamu, and Santhal Pargana. In this soil, pebbles, acidic, iron oxide, aluminum, manganese oxide are found.
Black soil - This soil is found maximum in the Rajmahal range (Santhal-Parganas), it is found in this soil containing iron, chuna, magnesium, salty, fertile etc.
