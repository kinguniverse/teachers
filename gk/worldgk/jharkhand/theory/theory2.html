Important Notes of Jharkhand Power Project

Tenughat Thermal Power Plant ---- This power project was established in 1953 and has a generating capacity of up to 450 MW.
Bokaro Thermal Power Plant ---- This power project was established in 1955 and it has a generation capacity of up to 830 MW.
Chandrapura Thermal Power Plant ---- This power project was established in 1966 and has a generation capacity of up to 780 MW.
Damodar Valley Hydro Electric Project ---- This power project was established in 1948 and has a generation capacity of up to 104 MW.
Swarnrekha Hydroelectric Station ---- This power project was established in 1982-83 and it has a generation capacity of up to 130 MW.
Konar Hydroelectric Station ---- This power project was established in 1955 and it has a generation capacity of up to 40000 KW
Bokaro Hydroelectric Powerhouse ---- This power project was established in 1967 and it has a generation capacity of up to 385 MW.
Maithon Hydroelectric Station ---- This power project was established in 1958 and has a generation capacity of up to 7600 KW
