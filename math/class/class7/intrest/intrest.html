Find the simple interest of the following questions.

(a) If the interest of Rs 100 is 5 in 1 year. What will be the interest of Rs 200 in 1 year.
(b) The interest of Rs 600 is Rs 60 in 1 year. What will be the interest of Rs 900 in 1 year.
(c) The simple interest of Rs 800 in 1 year is Rs 80. What will be the interest of Rs 800 in 5 years.
(d) The simple interest of Rs 700 in 1 year is Rs 30. What will be the simple interest of Rs 1400 in 3 years.




(a) The interest of Rs 100 is 5 in 1 year.
Interest of Rs.1 in one year = 5/100 = 1/20
Interest of Rs 200 in 1 year 200x1/20 = 10

(b) The interest of Rs 600 is 60 in 1 year.
Interest of Rs.1 in one year = 60/600 = 1/10
Interest of Rs 900 in 1 year 900x1/10 = 90

(c) The interest of Rs 800 is 80 in 1 year.
Interest of Rs.1 in one year = 80/800 = 1/10
Interest of Rs 800 in 5 year 5x800x1/10 = 400

(d) The interest of Rs 700 is 30 in 1 year.
Interest of Rs.1 in one year = 30/700 = 3/70
Interest of Rs 1400 in 3 year 3x1400x3/70 = 180

(a) Principal = 400, Time = 2 years, Rate = 3% p.a.
(b) Principal = 600, Time = 4 years, Rate = 6% p.a.
(c) Principal = 320, Time = 5 years, Rate = 3 x 1/2% p.a.
(d) Principal = 550, Time = 3 years, Rate = 5% p.a.
(e) Principal = 450, Time = 2 years, Rate = 4% p.a.

simple interest = Principal x rate x time/100

simple interest = Principal x rate x time/100

600x6x4/100 = 144

(c) Principal = 320, Time = 5 years, Rate = 3 x 1/2% p.a.
rate = 3x1/2 = 7/2

(d) Principal = 550, Time = 3 years, Rate = 5% p.a.
simple interest = Principal x rate x time/100
550x5x3/100 = 165/2 = 82.5

(e) (e) Principal = 450, Time = 2 years, Rate = 4% p.a.
simple interest = Principal x rate x time/100

450x4x2/100 = 36

(a) Rs.600 for 4 years at 3% interest per annum
(b) Rs.1500 for 146 days at 5% per annum interest
(c) Rs.550 for 3 years at 10% per annum interest
(d) Rs.1200 for 3 years at 12% per annum interest




(a) Principal = 600, Time = 4 years, Rate = 3% p.a.
simple interest = Principal x rate x time/100

600x3x4/100 = 72
