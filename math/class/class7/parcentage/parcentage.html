A Bucket contained 10 liters of water. Due to the hole, 8 liters of water is released. Find the percentage of water left.



Answer : 25%

Water comes out from the hole = 8 liters

Water left in the bucket = 10 liters - 8 liters = 2 liters

Percentage of water = Remaining water x 100 / Initial water

2x100/8 = 25%
