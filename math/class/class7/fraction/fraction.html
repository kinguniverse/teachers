Compare the following fractions using the signs '>', '=' ', '<'.

(a) 7/8 and 3/5



Answer : 7/8 > 3/5


LCM of 8 and 5 = 2x2x2x5 = 40

7/8 = 7/8x5/5 = 35/40

We know 35 > 24 .

35/40 > 24/40 or 7/8 > 3/5
