In a freezing process, it needs to be reduced at a rate of 40 °C to 5 °C per hour to operate at room temperature. What will be the temperature of the room after 10 hours of starting this process



Answer : -10°C


Initial temperature of room = 40°C
Hourly temperature will be lower = -5°C

So the temperature of ten hours is = (-5°C ) x 10°C= -50°C

room temperature after ten hours = 40°C-50°C= -10°C

There are total 15 questions in an exam. 4 marks are awarded for each correct answer and -2 marks for the wrong answer.
(a) Saraswati solves all the questions. But out of their answers only 9 are correct. how many marks did he get in total



Answer : 24


Total number of questions = 15
Total number of correct answer = 9
So number of wrong answer = 15-9 = 6
Marks awarded for correct answer = 9 x 4 = 36
Marks awarded for wrong answer = 6 x -2 = -12

So the marks obtained by Saraswati = 36 -12 = 24

A fertilizer company makes a profit of Rs.8 per bag on selling urea fertilizer and makes a loss of Rs.5 per bag on selling DAP fertilizer. (a) In a month the company sells 2000 bags of Urea and 3000 bags of DAP. find profit or loss



Answer : 1000 rupees profit


Profit of selling urea fertilizer = 8
Loss of selling DAP manure = -5
Profit on 2000 bags of urea = 2000 x 8 = 16000
Loss on 3000 DAP bag = 3000 x -5 =-15000

Manure sold = 16000 -15000 = 1000

Hence the company made a profit of Rs. 1000
