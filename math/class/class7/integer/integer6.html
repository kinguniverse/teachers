A shopkeeper makes a profit of Re 1 by selling a pen. And while selling his old stock pencils there is a loss of 40 paise per pencil.

(a) In the month of October, he suffered a loss of Rs.5. During this 45 pens were sold. Find how many pencils he sold during this period



Answer : 125 pencil


loss to shopkeeper = 5
profit on selling a pen = 1

profit on selling 45 pens = 45x1 = 45

The total amount with the shopkeeper = 45-(-5) = 50
50 rupees = 5000 paise

loss of selling a pencil = 40 paise

Total number of pencils sold = 5000 / 40 =125
