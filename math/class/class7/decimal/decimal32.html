Find the area of a rectangle. Whose length is 6.5 cm and width is 4.2 cm.



Answer : 27.3


Length of rectangle = 6.5 cm
Width of rectangle = 4.2 cm

Area of rectangle = length x width
6.5x4.2 = 27.3 sq cm

A car travels 15.50 km per liter of petrol. How far will 12.5 liters of petrol go?



Answer : 193.75 km


Distance covered in one liter of petrol = 15.50 km.
So the distance covered in 12.5 liters of petrol = 12.5x15.50 = 193.75 km
