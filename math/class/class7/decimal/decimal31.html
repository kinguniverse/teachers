John bought a notebook for Rs 45.50, a pen for Rs 15.25 and a pencil for Rs 05.75. He gave a note of Rs 100 to the shopkeeper. Tell how much money he will get back.



Answer : 33.5


John gave to the shopkeeper = Rs 100.
total value of goods purchased = 45.50 + 15.25 + 05.75 = 66.50

will get back = 100 - 66.50 = 33.5
