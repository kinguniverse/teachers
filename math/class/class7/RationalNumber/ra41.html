Fill in the correct symbols from >, <, and =.

(a) 4/6 7/9
(b) -3/4 -4/7
(c) 1/2 40/80
(d) 5/11 -5/11
(e) 0 -2/7




(a) 4/6 7/9

LCM of 6 and 9 = 18
On converting fraction to 18 denominator equivalent fraction

4/6 = 4/6x3/3 = 12/18

7/9 = 7/9x2/2 = 14/18

12/18 < 14/18

(b) -3/4 -4/7

LCM of 4 and 7 = 28
On converting fraction to 28 denominator equivalent fraction

-3/4 = -3/4x7/7 = -21/28

-4/7 = -4/7x4/4 = -16/28
-21/28 < -16/28

(c) 1/2 40/80

LCM of 2 and 40 = 80

On converting fraction to 80 denominator equivalent fraction

1/2 = 1/2x40/40 = 40/80

40/80 = 40/80x1/1 = 40/80

40/80 = 40/80
